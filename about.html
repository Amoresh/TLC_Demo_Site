About

TLC Express

Total Logistic & Courier Services Ltd.

TLC Express , the new low-cost courier in the city, offering multi module solutions for supplying your Export and Import needs with the best value-for-money express courier and parcel delivery service, providing customers up to 50% savings in costs compared to existing courier services.

TLC Express is designed to provide low cost courier services for businesses, average consumers, corporate clients and overseas foreign workers for the carriage of regular parcels, business packages and documents throughout the network of different Airlines. We ensure you that your global trade will be more efficient, reliable and most importantly cost-effective. Customers may call TLC Express Customer Service to arrange for pick-up of their consignments. Alternatively, customers can choose to drop their parcels at any of the TLC Express outlets or drop-off locations.

TLC Express , customers will enjoy swift, consistent, and timely deliveries of their parcels and shipments with services such as Door-to-Door Pickup and Deliveries, Customs clearance, Paperwork and Documentation Real Time Tracking & Tracing of your parcel and Proof of Delivery. Customers may keep track of their shipments by using the Tracking & Tracing feature online, by keying in the consignment, airway bill number provided.

TLC Express have skilled manpower who can handle your shipment in right way & committed to better service.

We never compromise with our service level because service is our first priority. Our motto is “Committed To Better Service”.
