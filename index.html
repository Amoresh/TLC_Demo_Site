Web Based Tracking
TLC Express online tracking is easy to find out the status and the position of your shipment. Use our online tracking system by entering your consignment number. Our time to time online tracking information can give you a clear images about your shipment & saving your time and eliminating hassles.

24/7 Business assistance
To track the TLC Express shipment status you should have the Consignment number with you. These numbers can be found on the acknowledgment receipt copy which you have received from the TLC Express at the time of booking the shipment. Enter these numbers in the tracking box and click on the tracking button to get the real-time status of your shipment.
